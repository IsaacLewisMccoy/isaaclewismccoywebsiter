A-342 is a large bipedal humanoid creature found near Gusau Nigeria. People in Gusau have named it Iyipada. The creature stands at 7.4ft tall. A-342 looks similar to a human, apart from its distinct black fur with stripes and creature also has elongated arms that reach down to their knees, the creature’s head is what personal noticed first. The head is capable of changing, sightings before capture have described heads of animals like monkeys, goats, and even some animals outside of Africa like a cockatoo and bear. In its confinement, these heads have been reported. But researchers are not sure if it is capable of other shifts. Reports of A-342 date back to mid 1600’s implying it is an ancient animal. If not species

Overall A-342 is a very violent creature and is to be kept in high security at all times. A-342 is not capable of speech and any form of speech to them is responded with deathly screams and violent outbursts. Researchers are unsure as to why these outbursts happen. But further research will be conducted
